About Paula

I'm a People Strategy and HR Business Partner with 12+ years building the workforce systems, performance frameworks, and talent infrastructure that scaling organizations need — and advising the senior leaders who depend on them.

I've built people functions from zero, navigated hypergrowth and restructuring, and partnered with C-suite leaders across global enterprises, scaling startups, and matrixed organizations supporting workforces of up to 20,000 employees. Whether the challenge is standing up a talent architecture that's never existed, or inheriting a broken one, I show up with a clear point of view and a bias toward action.

I hold an M.S. in Human Capital Analytics & Technology from NYU, a SHRM-CP, and Scrum certifications (CSM & CSPO) — and I bring a product mindset to people work: building for adoption, iterating based on feedback, and delivering outcomes that hold.
