About Paula

I'm a People & Talent leader who sits at the intersection of talent management and learning & development — not choosing between the two, but using both to drive organizational performance.

My work is grounded in systems thinking: how roles connect to capability, how capability connects to culture, and how culture connects to outcomes. I've built programs from zero, fixed ones that weren't working, and partnered with executives to navigate complexity with clarity.

Whether greenfield or inherited, I bring a product mindset to people work — designing solutions that are user-centered, outcomes-driven, and built to scale.
